When brought on as General VA, the operation was carrying over 10,000 unverified lead records spread across disconnected platforms — no deduplication process, no SOP framework, and billing corrections handled entirely by memory. Scheduling conflicts were routine, appointment overlaps went unresolved, and new staff had nothing documented to reference during onboarding.

// THE APPROACH

Conducted a full end-to-end CRM audit, eliminating duplicate and stale entries across the entire lead database. Used Google Gemini to engineer a complete SOP library covering inbox management, calendar protocols, billing workflows, and escalation procedures. Designed a timezone-aware scheduling system to eliminate appointment overlap. Implemented a structured financial reconciliation process for billing, refunds, and membership cancellations.

// THE OUTCOME

Administrative overhead dropped by 40%. Billing accuracy reached 100% with zero refund errors logged. Customer satisfaction scores climbed to 98%. New staff onboarding time was reduced fivefold. The client's back-office now runs on documented, repeatable systems — not individual memory or improvisation.
